Welcome to OpenBreastUS

OpenBreastUS, a large-scale wave equation dataset designed to bridge the gap between theoretical equations and practical imaging applications. This collection contains over 16 million frequency-domain wave simulations derived from anatomically realistic human breast phantoms, developed by PKU Computational Scientific Imaging Lab.

Breast Dataset

  • 8,000 phantoms with 16,384,000 wavefields
